Wild School Experience

About Us

Wild School Experience is a company founded by Sandra Schleier, an environmentalist with a passion for scientific education. Our mission is to inspire the next generation to appreciate and protect our natural world through immersive, hands-on learning experiences in one of the most biodiverse ecosystems on the planet—El Yunque Rainforest.

With over a decade of experience in Marine Biology and Environmental Science, Sandra brings a wealth of knowledge and a deep commitment to conservation education. Her expertise and enthusiasm have made Wild School Experience a trusted name among educators and nature enthusiasts alike.

We believe that learning extends beyond the classroom, and by exploring the natural world, students can develop a lifelong connection with the environment. Whether it’s observing nocturnal wildlife or studying the complex ecosystems within the forest, our experiences are designed to ignite curiosity and foster a sense of responsibility toward the environment.

Services

We offer a variety of educational tours tailored specifically for middle and high school students. Our tours are an excellent opportunity for students to step out of their comfort zones and engage with nature in a way that is both educational and adventurous.

Our signature service is the Nighttime Rainforest Exploration, where students can experience the unique ecosystem of the only Tropical Rainforest in the U.S. Forest System under the cover of darkness. Guided by Sandra Schleier, students will learn about the nocturnal habits of rainforest creatures, the importance of biodiversity, and the ecological significance of the rainforest.

In addition to our rainforest tours, we also offer custom educational programs that align with school curricula, focusing on topics such as ecology, conservation, and the impacts of climate change on tropical ecosystems. Each program is designed to be interactive, engaging, and informative, ensuring that students leave with a deeper understanding and appreciation for the environment.

Our tours are available year-round, rain or shine, and are suitable for students of all ages. We also accommodate school groups, scout troops, and other educational organizations looking for a unique outdoor learning experience.
